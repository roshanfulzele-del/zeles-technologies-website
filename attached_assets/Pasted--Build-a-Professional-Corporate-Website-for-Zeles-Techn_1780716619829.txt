## About the Company

Zeles Technologies is a technology consulting and services company delivering Cybersecurity, Cloud, Infrastructure, Data Protection, and Software Development solutions. The company serves enterprises, SMEs, cooperative banks, healthcare organizations, educational institutions, and government organizations.

The founder has 18+ years of experience in IT Infrastructure, Networking, Cloud Security, Data Security, DLP, SIEM, SASE, Zero Trust Architecture, and Managed Security Services.
